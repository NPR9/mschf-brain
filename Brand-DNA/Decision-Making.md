## Decision-Making

**Flat and fast.** No layers. Candor replaces committees.

> "We've wiped away a lot of the bureaucracy... there's just so much candor here and like quick honesty and like ability to make fast decisions."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

**Instinct-based (on ideas):**
> "If you spend too much time deliberating on it, you're wrong."
> — Gabe, *30 MAY 2024 ART OF GOING VIRAL*

**Data: track but don't celebrate.** MSCHF ruthlessly tracks open rates, sellout times, resell multiples, video views — but internally it's forbidden to use that data as the emotional measure of success.

> "We would ruthlessly track open rates, sellout times, resell multiples, page views, video views on content that other people make. That was a very, very important part. Couldn't celebrate that though."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

**Worst practices protected.** Finance wants best practices. MSCHF protects space for financially irrational projects because they produce cultural returns that compound.

> "There's like a target of money that you need to make to be able to do like bigger things that just don't make money but are so cool that you have to do them."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*
