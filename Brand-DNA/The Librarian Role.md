## The Librarian Role

One full-time person whose job is to feed raw material into the Mischief Machine. Unique to MSCHF. Not a creative director — an input curator.

> "We have one person whose full-time job is essentially, it's very similar to being a university professor or a high school teacher. We refer to them as the librarian. Their job is to curate topics, build a curriculum, do research, and present new inputs for the Mischief Machine. And that comes in the form of white papers, mood boards, presentations, guest lectures, talks, anything that you can imagine, just like you would see in school. Those are circulated on a weekly basis to the team."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*
