## What MSCHF Actually Is

MSCHF resists definition on purpose. But across all transcripts, a consistent picture emerges:

> "It's me and 30 of my friends here in Brooklyn. And we've basically created a wedge in the universe that allows us to make whatever we want, whenever we want, that somehow conveys our point of view in ways that are actually interactive in the systems that we're commenting on."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

> "Mischief is whatever is most convenient for you to describe it as at the time... in court in front of a judge it behooves mischief to 100% unabashedly be an artistic entity. The convenient thing is that it is also true."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

**Formal structure:** For-profit art collective. Represented by Perrotin Gallery. Works in museums worldwide. Also sells physical goods, software, and luxury items. Raised VC money from Silicon Valley.

**Current structure (2025):** Holding company of creative enterprises. Each category (footwear, luxury leather goods, fine art, potentially theme parks) gets its own sub-brand, team, budget, P&L. Example: GSCT by MSCHF (handbag line).

**Confidence:** HIGH
