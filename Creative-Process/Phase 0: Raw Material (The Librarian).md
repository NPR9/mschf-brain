## Phase 0: Raw Material (The Librarian)

Before any brainstorm happens, one full-time person curates inputs.

> "We have one person whose full-time job is essentially like being a university professor or a high school teacher. We refer to them as the librarian. Their job is to curate topics, build a curriculum, do research, and present new inputs for the Mischief Machine. And that comes in the form of white papers, mood boards, presentations, guest lectures, talks, anything that you can imagine. Those are circulated on a weekly basis to the team."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

**Why it matters:** Ideas don't start from "what's trending." They start from curated material — cultural, academic, technical. This is what prevents MSCHF from being reactive.
