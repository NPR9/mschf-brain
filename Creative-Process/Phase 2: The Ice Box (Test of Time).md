## Phase 2: The Ice Box (Test of Time)

Ideas that survive initial brainstorm go into a pool. Then they're deliberately ignored.

> "We stow it away for many months and then we revisit it later. If it still hits for us, then we will start committing resources to doing it."
> — Gabe, *15 Oct 2025 outlasting hype*

> "We forced the idea to sit in that pool for another couple of months. Now it has to survive the test of time. You forget about it, and you come back to it. And if it still feels exciting, then you slot it for production."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

> "Ideas have to sit in the pool for months at a time and every time we revisit the pool to add new ideas you have to kill old ones."
> — Gabe, *30 MAY 2024 ART OF GOING VIRAL*

**Vetting criteria:**
- Does it conceptually represent what we care about right now?
- Is it doable? Can we afford it?
- Can we distribute it?
- Does it still excite us 2–6 months later?

**Timeframe:** 2–6 months in the pool before green-light.

**Confidence:** HIGH
