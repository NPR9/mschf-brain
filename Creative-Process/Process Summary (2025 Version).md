## Process Summary (2025 Version)

```
WEEKLY: Librarian curates inputs → circulates white papers, mood boards, guest lectures

DAILY (Mon–Fri): 1-hour brainstorm → every idea documented + tagged

ONGOING: Mass pool of ideas → vetting (conceptually right? doable? distributable?)
         ↓
         Ice box (2–6 months ignored, then revisited)
         ↓
         Green light → Distro designs viral loops into product
         ↓
         Production (12 months out)
         ↓
         Pre-release celebration (night before)
         ↓
         Leak → Build tension → Release → Death strategy
         ↓
         Audience carries it across finish line
```
