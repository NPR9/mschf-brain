## The Idea Quality Test

**The One Sentence + Three Sentence Rule:**
> "A good idea has to hit in one sentence and hit even harder in three sentences. What that means is there are layers of comprehension. And typically those layers means it applies to multiple audiences."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

**The Eureka Pattern:**
When a good idea is heard, it feels obvious — like it already should exist.
> "When you see it exist, it makes sense. It's intuitive or obvious. You get why this should exist."
> — Gabe, *15 Oct 2025 outlasting hype* (on Tax Heaven 3000)

**Instant gut test on new ideas:**
> "If you spend too much time deliberating on it, you're wrong."
> — Gabe, *30 MAY 2024 ART OF GOING VIRAL*
