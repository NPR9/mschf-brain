## Digital / Experience Drops

### Insomnia Bot (Casper)
**Year:** ~2016 (first branded project)
**Concept:** SMS chatbot that only works 11pm–5am. Texts you about anything. Addresses loneliness without solving it.
**Quote:** "We created this weird sort of lackadaisical stream-of-consciousness bot that would literally talk to you about anything but it only works at a certain time... 11pm it would be like 'hey you up.'" — Gabe, *_16 Jan 2019*
**Principles:** [[Emotion First]], [[Cultural Ready-Made]] (chatbots were the cultural wave), [[Audience Completes the Work]]
**Confidence:** HIGH

### Times New Roman (Bigger Is Better)
**Year:** ~2017
**Concept:** Rebuilt the Times New Roman font 5–10% wider per character. Saves 2–3 pages on a 10-page paper. Undetectable to the naked eye.
**Quote:** "We took Times New Roman the beloved font of all of our past times and we deconstructed it... each character is roughly five to ten percent wider than its predecessor." — Gabe, *_16 Jan 2019*
**Principles:** [[Humor as Disarmament]], [[Spicy Present]], [[One Sentence + Three]]
**Confidence:** HIGH

### Persistence of Chaos
**Year:** ~2018
**Concept:** Air-gapped laptop containing 5–7 of the world's most destructive viruses (hundreds of billions in real-world damage). Sold at auction for $1.3M.
**Quote:** "It's just an air gapped laptop computer with I think like five to seven viruses on it that contributed to something like hundreds of billions of dollars of Destruction... it sold for $1.3 million." — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*
**Principles:** [[Cultural Ready-Made]], [[One Sentence + Three]] (benign object, catastrophic contents), [[Black Box]]
**Confidence:** HIGH

### Away Mode (Amazon Alexa skill)
**Year:** ~2018
**Concept:** Alexa skill that plays hours of banal, infuriating conversation to make your home seem occupied and too awkward for burglars. Inspired by Home Alone.
**Quote:** "We created a home security Alexa skill... that just played hours of infuriatingly banal boring conversations to make your home too awkward to deal with for burglars." — Gabe, *_24 Feb 2020 Hacking Culture*
**Principles:** [[Humor as Disarmament]], [[Cultural Ready-Made]]
**Confidence:** HIGH

### Tax Heaven 3000
**Year:** 2022–2023
**Concept:** Free TurboTax competitor as an anime dating simulator. You date "Iris" (named after the IRS) in a Japanese café. As the date gets more intimate, she asks your SSN. Fully functional tax software. #1 on Steam. 20,000 people used it to file taxes.
**Quote:** "It is a anime dating simulator where you go on a virtual date in a cute Anime Cafe with this girl her name is Iris after the IRS... she starts asking like the really personal stuff like what's your social security number." — Gabe, *30 MAY 2024 ART OF GOING VIRAL*
**Quote:** "The funniest things were going down online where people were basically split into two factions... one faction was 'for the love of God do not give the cute anime girl your social security number' and the other faction was 'dear Lord I'm risking everything for the cute anime girl.'" — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*
**Principles:** [[Cultural Ready-Made]] (anime × tax software), [[Humor as Disarmament]], [[One Sentence + Three]], [[Spicy Present]]
**Confidence:** HIGH
