## What Is MSCHF?

> "It's me and 30 of my friends here in Brooklyn. And we've basically created a wedge in the universe that allows us to make whatever we want, whenever we want, that somehow conveys our point of view in ways that are actually interactive in the systems that we're commenting on."
> — Gabe Whaley, 2025

For-profit art collective. Represented by Perrotin Gallery. Works in museums worldwide. Raises VC money. Sells physical goods, software, luxury items, and experiential art. Has been sued by Nike and multiple corporations. Wins on First Amendment grounds.

**The only honest answer:** Mischief is whatever is most convenient at the time. In court: art collective. On StockX: hype brand. In Silicon Valley: tech media play. In Paris: new luxury. All simultaneously correct and wrong.
