## 10. Creativity Is a Muscle, Not a Gift

**Definition:** Creativity requires daily training, not waiting for inspiration. MSCHF brainstorms every weekday. Volume of ideas is the mechanism that produces good ones.

**In their own words:**
> "Being creative is actually a muscle. It's not something that you're born with. If you go for long periods of time without stretching your brain in that way, you do get worse at it."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

> "Being creative doesn't mean you have good ideas. Being creative just means you're human. You have insights and you have reactions to things that are going on. Being creative means you're not afraid to look stupid."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 -Gabe Whaley on Why MSCHF Makes Virality an Art Form*

> "Every single day, Monday through Friday, there is a one-hour long brainstorm of various formats."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*
