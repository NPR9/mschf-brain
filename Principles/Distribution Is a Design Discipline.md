## 4. Distribution Is a Design Discipline

**Definition:** Where an idea appears is as important as the idea itself. MSCHF has a dedicated "distro" department that designs viral loops into ideas before production even starts.

**In their own words:**
> "We built a department at Mischief called the distribution. We call it distro. Distro is obsessed with how do you plant opportunities for natural circulation... building in loops like viral shareable loops into the production process itself."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

> "It's not enough to do the thing, you have to figure out where to put the thing... we refer to distribution as like how does an idea spread and how do you boil that down to like the most tactile like physical essence."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

> "A big part of our strategy used to be having a death strategy for every product... there's the get excited before it's real. Now it's real. Then it dies. Three moments."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

**Confidence:** HIGH

**Example:** [[Satan Shoes]] — Lil Nas X wearing them in Montero music video was Distro built into the product itself.
