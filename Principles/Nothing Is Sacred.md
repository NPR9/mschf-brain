## 25. Nothing Is Sacred

**Definition:** Any cultural, religious, commercial, or institutional system is available as raw material. No domain is off-limits on principle — the question is only whether the target is powerful enough to be worth punching (see Principle 23).

**Confidence:** HIGH [Exhibition title "Nothing Is Sacred" (Daelim Museum, Seoul, 2021) makes this explicit. Applied consistently: Jesus Shoes, Satan Shoes, Infinite Relic, Severed Spots all treat sacred institutions as creative material.]

**In their own words:**
> Title of MSCHF's exhibition at Daelim Museum, Seoul, 2021: *Nothing Is Sacred*

**How it relates to Principle 23:** "Nothing Is Sacred" describes what is available as material. "Always Punch Something, Never Punch Down" describes who can be targeted. Together they define the full creative brief: anything is usable, but the target must deserve it.

**Proves it:** [[Drops/jesus-shoes]] (Catholic iconography as sneaker material), [[Drops/infinite-relic]] (saint's blood as biotech project), [[Drops/severed-spots]] (Damien Hirst as raw material), [[Drops/satan-shoes]] (the most charged religious iconography in Western culture)
