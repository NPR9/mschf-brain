## 7. Outsider Advantage

**Definition:** MSCHF's power comes from never having been insiders. Outsider perspective lets them see cultural systems clearly enough to remix them. Being accepted is the trap.

**In their own words:**
> "All of us come from such different backgrounds... we were all sort of like these Outsiders throughout our childhoods... all the work that we create is a commentary on what we're seeing on the inside without us wanting to even be accepted at the end of the day. And that's what gives us so much power to continue."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

> "There is a pull for mischief to like start to be more embedded into that institution... and I think that's the biggest trap... the good thing is everyone here is just inherently extremely lazy so it takes a lot of work to be on the inside and no one here actually wants to do it."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

> "I think a lot of our advantage comes from our outsiderness."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*
