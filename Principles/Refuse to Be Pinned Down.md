## 3. Refuse to Be Pinned Down

**Definition:** The moment MSCHF becomes legible as one category, creative freedom dies. Deliberate category-ambiguity is both strategy and survival.

**In their own words:**
> "We didn't want to be able to be pinned down... the moment we're pinned down and have to do one thing and people know you only for this one object you're just going to have to keep doing that and then you lose that ability to be creative and free."
> — Lucas, *_19 Mar 2024 BUSINESS OF HYPE*

> "It's the best when so many people have a different opinion of what mischief is, because that means it's working. That means we haven't put ourselves in a box yet."
> — Gabe, *oct 2025 Making MSCHF, an Art and Discipline*

**Confidence:** HIGH
