## 2016: First Client, First Test

**Casper mattress** becomes founding client. Late Night Snack Packs (interactive video site simulating going-out scenes). Insomnia Bot (SMS chatbot, 11pm–5am only). Both become viral hits.
> "Casper had reached out and that was sort of the impetus... the next test was okay they paid for it, now we need to create it and make sure it works... Late Night Snack Packs was a huge hit and that was the first thing that we ever put out."
> — Gabe, *_16 Jan 2019 GoDaddy*

**Key realization:** Brands will pay for earned media if you can prove it works. But they still want safety over novelty.

**Confidence:** HIGH
