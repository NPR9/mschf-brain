## 2023: Big Red Boots + Peak Virality + Korean Retrospective

**Big Red Boots** drop (spring 2023). Originally ~200–300 pairs at $350. Single Instagram mirror pic goes viral. Algorithm + organic sharing simultaneously. 100,000+ orders within an hour. Black market fakes flood within weeks. Meta uses it as case study for digital marketers. Still their most culturally known drop.

**Micro Handbag** — microscopic, sells at $63,750 at Paris Fashion Week auction.

**GSCT Handbag** — telephone game across 5 factories. So successful it becomes its own sub-brand.

**Met Sink** — Ship of Theseus applied to the Metropolitan Museum. Piece technically in the Met bathroom.

**Korean Museum Retrospective** (November 2023). Breaks attendance records. K-pop stars, Squid Game cast. Gabe reconnects with Korean cultural identity.
> "There is actually like a shared international language of creativity... all those kids had so much in common actually with the kids that show up at our New York opening."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

**Post-Big-Red-Boots realization:** Went too close to the sun. Fakes flooded the market. MSCHF calls factories, ramps production, still can't compete with Shein. Active decision: don't be the Big Red Boot company. Don't be a shoe company.
> "We never wanted to be a big red boot company. We don't even want to be a shoe company."
> — Gabe, *9 Oct 2024 making MSCHF*

**Confidence:** HIGH
